About MICHRO

Intelligence, Architected.

MICHRO delivers sovereign, high-density compute infrastructure engineered for the Generative AI era. We enable enterprises to train, fine-tune, and deploy Foundation Models, Large Language Models (LLMs), and RAG pipelines behind their own firewall.

Our Mission

To empower enterprises with sovereign Generative AI infrastructure that breaks the memory bottleneck limiting Foundation Model training and LLM development. We believe that AI intelligence should be built on-premises, with zero data egress and complete data sovereignty.

Our platforms are engineered for Transformer architectures, Vector Databases, and RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) pipelines at enterprise scale.
